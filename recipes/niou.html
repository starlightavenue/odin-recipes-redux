🪄 Niou Masaharu | 仁王雅治 🪄

Meet my little paradox, my little trickster, my first star.

When we were first introduced to him, his moveset was a mystery. It stayed a mystery for the entirety of the first season, but I felt in my heart of hearts that he was meant to be a star boy.

When he unveiled his greatest trick, I don't think anyone was more excited than I was to see his Meteor Drive.

I love everything about him, from his puri's to his pupiina's. He's such a sweetheart, and I'm glad that he's finally chosen himself.

Here's how to make the star boy that's always ten steps ahead of his captive audience:

🐣 Ingredients 🐣

  • Heat Intolerance
  • Love of Architecture
  • Penchant for Stray Cats
  • Vocal Tics
  • Charm Point
  • Unbridled Whimsy
  • Trickster Code
  • Undiagnosed Autism
  • Cute Hairpins
  • An Honest Heart
  • P Kimochi

🦊 Instructions 🦊

  1. Win a tennis tournament while disguised as your big sister.
    • CAUTION: This will awaken something in you.
  2. Switch places with your gentleman of a doubles partner.
  3. Cement your place as the Trickster of the Courts.
    • CAUTION: Nobody will view you the same way ever again.
  4. Choose yourself over a victory.
    • WARNING: YOUR CAPTAIN CANNOT BEAT THE TWELVE YEAR OLD.
  5. Lay low for a few months.
  6. Lose to your "gentleman" of a doubles partner.
    • CAUTION: Do not climb the mountain.
  7. Climb the mountain.
  8. Choose victory over yourelf.
    • WARNING: NOBODY WILL VIEW YOU THE SAME WAY EVER AGAIN.
  9. Switch places with a suprisingly benevelolent king.
    • REMINDER: It doesn't have to be like this.
  10. Reflect on what you want in life.
  11. Put on the greatest show the world has ever seen.
    • REMINDER: You are the trickster of the courts. You win on your own terms.
  12. Choose yourself as dramatically as possible
      13. CAUTION: Crashouts, regardless of how cultivated they are, are ill-advised.
  13. Travel the world, reaffirm your P Kimochi.
      14. REMINDER: Living life with an honest heart is what's best.
