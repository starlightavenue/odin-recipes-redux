Introducing my sunshine, my pookie, my light in the dark.

When I first saw Luke and his sunshine yellow color scheme, I couldn't help but smile.

When I found out he was a detective who loved photography and fixing old antiques, my heart melted.

When I found out his golden retriever personality was hiding something darker, something more complex, I felt seen.

The more I learned about his story and his struggles, the more I loved him. His light is so bright despite how bleak things get, and it's so admirable. As his story developed, we grew together, and his hope gives me hope.

Here's how to make a sunflower that can grow in even the coldest winters:
